Featured Project

Inventory Optimization & Decision Support

Operations Research · Inventory Policy Design · Python Analytics

Development of an inventory optimization framework for replenishment policy design under shortage risk, holding costs, service-level targets, lead times, minimum order quantities, and warehouse constraints.

  • Designed replenishment rules and target inventory logic across a product portfolio.
  • Calculated safety stock and target stock parameters aligned with reliability KPIs.
  • Implemented demand segmentation for regular, irregular, and seasonal demand patterns.
  • Built a decision-support framework for practical replenishment decisions in a constrained operating environment.

Additional Projects

Production Workflow Optimization

Applied Mathematics · Sequencing Optimization · Operational Efficiency

Development of optimization models for sequential-parallel production workflows to improve end-to-end processing efficiency.

  • Applied greedy-algorithm logic to production sequencing.
  • Focused on practical improvement of throughput and workflow coordination.
  • Reduced end-to-end order processing lead time by 50% through sequencing optimization for sequential-parallel production workflows.

Warehouse Footprint Optimization

Storage Optimization · Cost Reduction · Space Constraints

Optimization of warehouse placement and consolidation decisions under space constraints and operating requirements.

  • Developed consolidation logic for warehouse footprint reduction.
  • Reduced warehouse holding and storage costs by 30%.
  • Combined warehouse consolidation decisions with inventory policy design and reduced warehouse holding costs by 30%.

Python-Based Scenario Analysis Tool

Python · Sensitivity Analysis · Cost-Risk Trade-offs

Development of a Python-based analytical tool for scenario analysis, sensitivity testing, and replenishment parameter recommendations.

  • Supported scenario modeling and sensitivity testing.
  • Compared trade-offs between cost, risk, and service-level decisions.
  • Supported decision-makers with scenario comparison, sensitivity testing, and cost-risk trade-off analysis.

Railway Embankment Thermal Modeling

Thermal Modeling · Permafrost Soils · Infrastructure Engineering

Thermal modeling of railway embankment foundation soils under changing snow-cover conditions.

  • Focused on thermal behavior in infrastructure foundations under changing snow-cover conditions.
  • Connected engineering analysis with mathematically structured process modeling.
  • This project is connected with the 2026 publication on railway embankment degradation of permafrost soils under varying snow-cover conditions.
