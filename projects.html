Research and Applied Work

Projects

Selected work spanning optimization, industrial analytics, numerical modeling, and engineering research.

Inventory Optimization and Replenishment Analytics

Applied Mathematics | Inventory Policy Design | Python Analytics

Designed an inventory optimization framework balancing shortage risk against holding costs across a product portfolio. Developed replenishment rules that accounted for lead times, minimum order quantities, warehouse capacity constraints, and target service levels.

Built a Python-based analytical instrument for scenario modeling, cost-risk trade-off evaluation, sensitivity testing, and replenishment parameter recommendations.

The project supported data-driven inventory decisions and operational planning under uncertainty.

Production Workflow Optimization

Operations Research | Sequencing Logic | Process Optimization

Developed optimization models for sequential-parallel production workflows and improved order execution logic. The implemented approach reduced end-to-end order processing lead time by 50%.

Warehouse Footprint and Storage Cost Optimization

Constraint-Based Optimization | Warehouse Analytics

Developed a consolidation algorithm accounting for warehouse space constraints and placement efficiency. This work contributed to a 30% reduction in storage-related costs.

Numerical Modeling of Seasonal Heat Transfer in Railway Embankments

Finite Difference Methods | Thermal Modeling | Railway Infrastructure

Developed a 2D transient heat-conduction model for railway embankments with sensitivity analysis for snow cover, climate parameters, and soil properties.

The project supports assessment of seasonal thermal effects and embankment stability in cold-region railway systems. It also includes planned verification of the numerical scheme through convergence and error-estimation analysis.

Computational and Experimental Research in Nuclear Engineering

Thermal-Hydraulics | Reactor Materials | Scientific Research

Conducted long-term computational and experimental work related to fuel behavior, irradiation effects, thermal reliability, and structural-material performance in reactor environments.

Prepared analytical reports, scientific publications, and conference materials; participated in validation-related work involving thermal-physics and mechanics codes.

Technical Consulting and Scientific Communication

Consulting | Training | Cross-Functional Communication

Delivered expert consulting, lectures, and technical presentations on nuclear technologies and related analytical topics. Adapted complex scientific content for both technical and non-technical audiences and supported English-language communication.
