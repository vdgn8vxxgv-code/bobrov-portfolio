Featured Project

Inventory Optimization & Decision Support

Applied Mathematics À Operations Research À Python Analytics À Supply Chain Optimization

A practical industrial project focused on inventory policy design, replenishment logic, warehouse efficiency, and scenario-based decision support. The work combined mathematical modeling with Python-based analytics to improve both operational performance and decision quality.

  • Reduced end-to-end order processing lead time by 50%.
  • Reduced warehouse holding and storage costs by 30%.
  • Built analytical tools for scenario analysis, sensitivity testing, and parameter recommendations.
  • Integrated lead times, MOQ, capacity constraints, and service-level logic into replenishment decisions.

This is the strongest example of how I connect applied mathematics, optimization, and business impact in a real operating environment.
