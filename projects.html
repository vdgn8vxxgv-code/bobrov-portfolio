Research and Applied Work

Projects

Selected work spanning optimization, industrial analytics, numerical modeling, and engineering research.

Inventory Optimization and Replenishment Analytics

Applied Mathematics | Inventory Policy Design | Python Analytics

Designed an inventory optimization framework balancing shortage risk against holding costs across a product portfolio. Developed replenishment rules incorporating lead times, minimum order quantities, warehouse capacity constraints, and target service levels.

Implemented demand segmentation for regular, irregular, and seasonal product groups and built a Python-based analytical tool for scenario modeling, sensitivity analysis, and cost-risk trade-off evaluation.

Production Workflow Optimization

Operations Research | Process Optimization | Decision Support

Developed optimization models for sequential-parallel production workflows and improved operations sequencing logic. The resulting approach reduced end-to-end order processing lead time by 50%.

Warehouse Footprint and Storage Cost Optimization

Constraint-Based Optimization | Warehouse Analytics

Designed a consolidation and placement methodology under warehouse space constraints. This work supported a 30% reduction in storage-related costs through improved allocation logic and footprint optimization.

Numerical Modeling of Seasonal Heat Transfer in Railway Embankments

Finite Difference Methods | Thermal Modeling | Railway Infrastructure

Developed a 2D transient heat-conduction model for railway embankments using finite-difference methods, including sensitivity analysis for snow cover, climate parameters, and soil properties.

The project supports engineering assessment of seasonal thermal effects and embankment stability in cold-region railway infrastructure.

Computational and Experimental Research in Nuclear Engineering

Thermal-Hydraulics | Reactor Materials | Scientific Research

Conducted long-term computational and experimental research in fuel behavior, irradiation effects, thermal reliability, and reactor-material performance. Prepared analytical reports, publications, conference presentations, and technical materials supporting validation-related work.
