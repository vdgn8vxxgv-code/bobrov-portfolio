Numerical Modeling of Seasonal Heat Transfer in Railway Embankments

Numerical methods | Heat transfer | Railway infrastructure | Python

Developed a 2D transient heat-conduction model using finite-difference methods for thermal analysis of railway embankments. The model includes sensitivity analysis for snow cover, climate parameters, and soil properties.

This work supports assessment of seasonal thermal effects and embankment stability in cold-region railway infrastructure.
