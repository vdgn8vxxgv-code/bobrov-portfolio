Research Profile

My research background is rooted in applied mathematics, numerical modeling, engineering analysis, and technical decision support. Over time, this foundation has expanded toward optimization, industrial analytics, and practical methods for solving complex operational problems.

This page presents the main thematic areas of my publication and research work rather than a full bibliographic archive. It is designed to show continuity between scientific rigor, analytical modeling, and applied industrial problem-solving.

Research Themes

Applied Mathematics & Numerical Modeling

Mathematical Modeling · Numerical Methods · Analytical Frameworks

Work in mathematical formalization of technical systems, numerical analysis, and model-based interpretation of engineering and operational processes.

This area forms the core methodological foundation of my research profile and remains central to both scientific and industrial applications.

Engineering Analysis & Technical Systems

Engineering Research · Process Analysis · System Behavior

Research and analytical studies related to complex technical environments, engineering processes, system performance, and experimentally informed interpretation of operating conditions.

The emphasis is on technically rigorous analysis that can support understanding, validation, and improvement of real systems.

Optimization & Decision Support

Operations Research · Optimization Logic · Decision Analytics

A more recent strand of work connects research methods with practical optimization, analytical decision support, and structured evaluation of constrained alternatives.

This direction bridges academic methods with industrial implementation in inventory, production, and broader operational contexts.

Industrial Analytics & Applied Research

Industrial R&D · Applied Analytics · Scenario Analysis

Applied analytical work focused on turning technical complexity into usable decision frameworks for industrial and research organizations.

This includes scenario analysis, model-based reasoning, and the communication of analytical results to technical and managerial stakeholders.

Current Direction

My current professional trajectory connects long-term scientific and engineering research with applied mathematics, operations research, Python-based analytics, and optimization for industrial environments.

In this sense, the publication profile and the project portfolio belong to one coherent system: rigorous research methods on one side, practical decision support and measurable impact on the other.
