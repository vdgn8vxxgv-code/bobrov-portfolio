Selected Publication

Bobrov S.N., Kasatkin I.A., Voronov V.P., Siluta A.G. INFLUENCE OF SNOW COVER HEIGHT ON RAILWAY EMBANKMENT DEGRADATION OF PERMAFROST SOILS IN ITS FOUNDATION // INTELLECTUAL POTENTIAL OF RUSSIA 2026: Collection of articles based on the results of the All-Russian scientific and practical conference with international participation (Orenburg, Russian Federation, March 4, 2026). – Sterlitamak: AMI, 2026. – 4-13 pp.

Thermal Modeling · Railway Embankments · Permafrost Soils · Engineering Analysis

This work addresses the influence of snow cover on thermal conditions and degradation processes in railway embankment foundation soils.

The publication reflects my continued interest in mathematically structured analysis of complex thermal and engineering processes.

Conference Presentations

Representative Conference and Seminar Topics

Nuclear Engineering · Reactor Materials · Fuel Rod Testing · Engineering Analysis

  • Thermal reliability of the SM-3 reactor core under heat transfer crisis conditions.
  • Fission gas release in VVER-1000 fuel rods with high burn-up under power ramp conditions.
  • In-reactor testing of fuel rods and reactor materials in RIAR research reactors.
  • Modeling of technological processes in nuclear power plant environments.
  • Validation-related analytical work involving RAPTA and RELAP5/mod3 engineering codes.

These topics reflect the main technical directions of my earlier research presentations, analytical reports, and reactor-related studies.

Manuscripts in Preparation

Inventory Optimization Under Lead Time, MOQ, and Capacity Constraints

Inventory Optimization · Operations Research · Supply Chain Analytics

A manuscript in preparation on replenishment policy design under lead times, minimum order quantities, warehouse constraints, and service-level targets.

The paper is intended to summarize practical optimization logic for target stock, safety stock, and replenishment decision rules across a product portfolio.

Scenario Analysis and Python-Based Decision Support for Replenishment Policy Design

Python Analytics · Scenario Analysis · Decision Support Systems

A manuscript in preparation on Python-based analytical tools for scenario comparison, sensitivity testing, and cost-risk trade-off evaluation in inventory management.

The focus is on making quantitative models directly usable for operational decision-making.

Demand Segmentation and Safety Stock Design for Irregular and Seasonal Demand

Stochastic Demand Modeling · Safety Stock · Service Levels

A manuscript in preparation on demand segmentation, safety stock calculation, and target inventory design for regular, irregular, and seasonal demand patterns.

This topic connects mathematical modeling with practical inventory control in constrained environments.
